    The 6 rules
    01
    If it isn't black, grey, white, or cream — it doesn't enter as clothing. Colour lives only in accessories.
    02
    Every new piece must pair with at least 3 items you already own before buying it.
    03
    One colour pop per outfit. Scarf OR earrings OR lip. Not all three at once.
    04
    Coats go over everything. Buy outerwear that fits over chunky knits.
    05
    Fit over fashion. A well-cut black trouser beats any trendy piece every single morning.
    06
    Natural fibres only: merino, cotton, linen, silk. They last longer and layer better.
